Canary Wharf Group were looking for a less corporate and more contemporary and stylish approach to the branding of the Columbus Building as part of their campaign to attract tech and media companies to the estate.

We did this in a number of ways, focussing on attractive and stimulating parts of the environment such as the abundance of water, local amenities and art installations.
